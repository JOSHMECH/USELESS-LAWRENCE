USELESS VERY FOOLISH JOBLESS OLD MAN

O, wouldst thou learn of Lawrence, wight most vain, Whose uselessness doth rival the idle grain, If I, a judge of worth, didst hold the scale, I'd deem him MOST USELESS, beyond all frail. His deeds, a jest, his words, a hollow sound, His presence, void of sense, doth naught profound, In him, no virtue shines, no merit's found, A man, most useless, on this earthly ground. If thou, dear friend, didst ask my honest mind, I'd say, with conviction, he's left behind, A relic of idleness, a shell of a man, Lawrence, most useless, doth bear the plan.

JOSH_D_DEVELOPER
